Autism
Autism, also called autism spectrum disorder (ASD), is a lifelong, developmental condition. It affects the way a person communicates, interacts and processes information. Learn more about autism, how it affects people, and how to live well with autism.

Young people and adults are also diagnosed with autism.

Being in a supportive environment makes a big difference to an autistic person’s wellbeing.
